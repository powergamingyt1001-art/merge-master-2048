Introduction

Local Storage

All game data (scores, coins, power-ups, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. We do not transmit this data to any server. Clearing your browser data will reset your game progress.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information. Since game data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
